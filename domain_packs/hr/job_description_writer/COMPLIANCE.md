# Compliance Considerations — Job Description Writer

**This pack drafts job descriptions for human review. Do not publish output without HR and legal approval.**

## Regulatory context (non-exhaustive)

- **Inclusive hiring laws** (e.g. NYC salary transparency, EEOC guidance): JDs may affect who applies; biased language can create legal exposure.
- **EU AI Act**: If JD generation influences hiring pipelines, assess whether it contributes to a high-risk recruitment system.
- **Québec / GDPR**: Personal data in team context fields must be handled lawfully.

## What this template provides

- Server-enforced `human_review_required` and `disclaimer` on every response.
- Prompt guidance for inclusive language (not a substitute for legal review).

## What operators must still implement

- HR/legal sign-off before posting.
- Bias review of competency matrix and screening rubric.
- PII minimisation in inputs (`team_context`, `culture_notes`).

**For research and demo only unless you complete a full compliance programme.**
